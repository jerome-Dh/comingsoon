Compact Calculator Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2024

Compact Calculator ("App") is developed by SkeleNews. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your information in connection with your use of our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from you while you use the Compact Calculator App. This means we do not collect data that can be used to identify you directly, such as your name, email address, phone number, or device ID.

Log Data

When you use the App, we may collect non-identifiable log data. This data may include information such as your device type, operating system version, screen size, the features you use within the App, and the date and time of your usage. We use this information for internal purposes only, such as analyzing App performance and identifying areas for improvement.

Third-Party Services

The App does not currently integrate with any third-party services that may collect your information. However, we reserve the right to integrate such services in the future. In that case, we will update this Privacy Policy to reflect the collection and use of data by those third-party services.

Use of Information

We may use the non-identifiable information collected through the App to:

  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience.
  • Generate anonymous usage statistics.
  • Identify and fix bugs or technical issues.

Disclosure of Information

We will not share your information with any third party without your consent, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • As required by law or to comply with a legal process.
  • To protect the rights and safety of ourselves or others.

Data Retention

We will only retain the non-identifiable information collected through the App for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through the App from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is ever completely secure.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. We will take steps to delete the information from our servers.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@skelenews.com.
